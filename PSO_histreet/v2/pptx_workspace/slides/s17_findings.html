Key Findings — 4 Pricing Insights

01

16 SKUs Are Underpriced vs Market

36% of PSO's retail SKUs are positioned more than 10% below market median. The highest gap: Carient Plus 10W-40 1L at −27.6%. This represents a direct margin recovery opportunity without volume risk.

02

5W-30 Commands a Disproportionate Premium

Carient Ultra 5W-30 is priced +127% above market median on 1L packs. While partly justified by spec premium, this outlier price may be suppressing trial. A 30–40% reduction is recommended.

03

DEO Tier Gap Is Too Narrow

DEO 8000 (super premium) is priced almost identically to DEO 6000 (premium). The inter-tier spread is only 2–4%. Widening to 10–15% would strengthen trade-up rationale for fleet managers.

04

MCO Blaze is Significantly Underpriced

Blaze 4T and Blaze Xtreme are both positioned 17–27% below market median on 1L packs. Given motorcycle oil's high repurchase frequency, selective price recovery here yields outsized revenue impact.
