F3 — Price-Pack Architecture (PPA)

WEIGHT: 20%

PACK PREMIUM LOGIC

Larger packs should carry a lower price-per-litre — rewarding bulk buyers while protecting per-unit margin on small packs. PPA enforces a consistent step-down curve.

STEP-DOWN RULE APPLIED

0.7L → 1L: flat premium (convenience premium justified)

BULK DISCOUNT APPLIED

1L → 3L: −8% per litre | 3L → 4L: −4% per litre
